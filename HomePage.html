Computer Class

Computer Class is a pet project of the Pineapple People Pty. aiming to

encourage and assist school students to learn about computers and their functions.

In a world of increasing dependence on computers in everyday life, a knowledge of how computers work,

how to program them and how to understand why they work is an important skill necessary for life in our technological age.

Computer Class intends to demonstrate how important these skills are and to provide the necessary knowledge to be able to make

computers work in the way that you want them and to make them work for you.
